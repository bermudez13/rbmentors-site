Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

At Right Business Mentors, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and contact details.
  • Tax and financial information voluntarily provided for tax preparation services, including documents such as W-2s, 1099s, and supporting records.
  • Website usage data, including IP address, browser type, and interaction data collected through security and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only for legitimate business purposes:

  • Preparing and filing tax returns accurately and in compliance with IRS requirements.
  • Communicating with you regarding inquiries, services, or your account.
  • Maintaining records required for compliance and future reference.
  • Protecting and improving our website and services.

3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

We apply industry-standard safeguards to protect your information, including secure cloud-based storage, encryption where applicable, and access controls limited to authorized personnel.

Internal procedures are designed to align with IRS data safeguarding guidelines. Your information is never stored publicly or shared without authorization.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Information may be disclosed only when:

  • Required by law or regulatory authorities.
  • You provide explicit consent.
  • Necessary to operate our services through trusted providers under confidentiality and security obligations.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to legal retention requirements.

Requests can be submitted by email to: rafael@rbmentors.com

6. Record Retention

Tax-related records are retained in accordance with IRS requirements. Records are generally kept for a minimum of three (3) years and, in some cases, up to seven (7) years depending on legal obligations.

7. Cookies and Website Technologies

Our website may use cookies, security tools, and limited analytics to protect against abuse, improve performance, and understand usage. You may disable cookies through your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be available on our website.
