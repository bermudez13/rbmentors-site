About Right Business Mentors

Tax preparation with an educational, ethical, and personalized approach, designed to support informed financial decision-making with confidence.

Who is behind Right Business Mentors

My name is Rafael Bermudez, and I am a tax preparer in the United States. Right Business Mentors was created based on firsthand experience working with individuals who, year after year, approach the tax process with questions, stress, or incomplete information.

Over time, it became clear that many individuals and small businesses require more than tax return preparation. They also need clear explanations, guidance, and a better understanding of how their financial decisions directly affect their tax situation.

For this reason, the approach goes beyond completing forms. The focus is on helping each client understand their tax position and make informed decisions, both in the short and long term.

About the firm

Right Business Mentors is an independent tax preparation firm serving individuals, families, self-employed professionals, and small businesses seeking organized growth and proper compliance with their tax obligations.

The name reflects the firm’s philosophy: not only to provide a service, but to act as a mentor—supporting clients through tax compliance, financial organization, and responsible planning over time.

Professional training and preparation

The service is grounded in professional tax preparation. I am a Professional Tax Preparer with specialized training in tax preparation and planning, allowing services to be delivered in alignment with IRS requirements and a strong focus on responsible compliance.

In addition, I have a background in software engineering, which brings a structured mindset, attention to detail, and secure, well-organized handling of client information.

Ongoing education and regulatory updates ensure that guidance remains aligned with current IRS regulations and established best practices.

  • IRS-registered tax preparer (PTIN holder)
  • Professional Tax Preparer (PTP) certification
  • Training in tax preparation and planning for individuals and small businesses
  • Annual continuing education in tax law
  • Experience with individual, self-employed, and small business returns

Principles and values

  • Transparency and clear communication
  • Confidential and secure handling of information
  • Client education as an integral part of the service
  • Strict adherence to IRS rules and guidelines
  • Trust-based, long-term client relationships

Who the service is designed for

The firm primarily works with individuals and small businesses seeking professional, responsible, and reliable tax preparation services:

  • Individuals with W-2 income
  • Families seeking to optimize their tax position
  • Self-employed professionals and 1099 contractors
  • Small businesses in early or growth stages
